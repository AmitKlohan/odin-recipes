Green-Salad

Description

Classic Indian Sliced Salad Recipe is a salad which has sliced cucumbers, carrots, tomatoes & onions

seasoned with salt, pepper and lemon. You will find this salad served in restaurants

and it makes a lovely accompaniment to any meal.

  • Tomato
  • carrots

  • lemon
  • cucumbers
  • Radish
